About EastEdge Engineering

Innovating Construction Materials Since 2024

EastEdge Engineering is a leading manufacturer of precision blockets, serving the construction industry with innovative and sustainable solutions. Our products are engineered to meet the highest standards of quality and durability.

EastEdge Engineering produced the most widely used concrete blocks in Ethiopia, measuring 20x20x40cm, 15x20x40cm, and 10x20x40cm. Builders have a tendency to favor these standard dimensions because they provide an excellent balance between structural stability and cost-effectiveness.

Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for construction projects in Eastern Ethiopia especially in Dire Dawa.

Our Blocket Products

Standard hollow Blocket

Our flagship product, designed for general construction applications with optimal strength and durability.

  • High compressive strength
  • Weather resistant
  • Easy installation
  • Available in multiple sizes
  • Sizes: 20x20x40cm, 15x20x40cm, 10x20x40cm
Price: 50 - 100 ETB per block

Reinforced Blocket

Enhanced with steel reinforcement for applications requiring extra strength and load-bearing capacity.

  • Steel reinforced core
  • Extreme load capacity
  • Seismic resistant
  • Long-term durability
  • Sizes: 20x20x40cm, 15x20x40cm, 10x20x40cm
Price: 50 - 100 ETB per block

Solid and Eco Blocket

Environmentally friendly option made with recycled materials without compromising on quality or strength.

  • Made from recycled materials
  • Reduced carbon footprint
  • LEED certification eligible
  • Maintains full strength
  • Sizes: 20x20x40cm, 15x20x40cm, 10x20x40cm
Price: 100 - 200 ETB per block

Our Manufacturing Process

Material Selection

We source only the highest quality raw materials from trusted suppliers, ensuring consistency and reliability.

Precision Mixing

Our automated mixing process ensures perfect consistency batch after batch, with computerized quality control.

Molding & Forming

Advanced hydraulic presses form blockets to exact specifications with minimal variance and maximum density.

Curing & Drying

Temperature-controlled curing chambers ensure optimal strength development and material stability.
